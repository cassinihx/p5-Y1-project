SynthWave Audio Player

This artpiece is an animated artwork inspired by the Synthwave Movement. Synthwave is a genre of music that takes it's inspiration from the soundtracks and content of 1980s films, video games and in general, it's pop culture. It uses a lot of synthesizers and drum machines and has a retro feel to it. It also has a visual style that is inspired by the 80s, often featuring neon lights, chrome and futuristic imagery. This can be seen in music videos, album covers and live performances.
